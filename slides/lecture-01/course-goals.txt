

Goals for the Course
====================

1. Introduce multivariate statistics from a geometric perspective, emphasizing the geometry of vector spaces.

2. Develop a good working knowledge of Python (a general purpose programming language), R (a statistical computing environment), and the Unix-like command line (de facto standard for building bioinformatics pipelines).

3. Provide a statistical and computational toolbox for dealing with complex data -- visualization, data wrangling, exploratory 

4. Provide the tools and knowledge to conduct reproducible computational and statistical research.
